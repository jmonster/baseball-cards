FOR XBOX ONE S users: In order to get the xbox to play Blu-Ray in 4k. You need to go into the Monitor settings under:

INPUT, Make Aspect Ratio ORIGINAL
Under FreeSync make it Extended or basic...

CONFIRM it is in 4k by:
1. play a video
2. press the Xbox button twice
3. Goto settings / Video & Audio
4. Go into the Video settings
5. If it says 4k, you are in 4k... If it says 1080p something is wrong.

Use game mode: for reduced latency

If you are using an nvidia card, make sure the freesync option is set to: off

4.0 out of 5 starsMonitor is great, stand is super cheap plastic.
November 7, 2017
Color: 27-Inch 4K UHD IPS MonitorVerified Purchase
The monitor itself is fantastic. The cheap-o plastic base that comes with it is pretty much garbage, though. Plan on buying a proper monitor stand to go with this!

I use this with my new 13" MacBook Pro and the colors are amazing. I was on the lookout for a 4k monitor, after having gotten used to sitting at a big old Apple Cinema Display. They don't make those anymore, so I began the hunt. This is by far the best rated 4k monitor for the price, hands down. The colors are fantastic, I have zero bleeding or display issues at all. The menus are easy to access and manipulate, which is always a plus. The tiny bezels are what really pushed this monitor over the competition for me. I sit with with my desk facing a window so I can enjoy the sunshine and I really like that the the bezels are really small, minimizing the amount of blocked view to my window.

The stand the monitor comes with is probably the only negative thing about it. I have it sitting on a standing desk, which means there's a little bit more movement to the desk then normal, and it's enough to cause the whole monitor to constantly wobble while I'm typing. There are decent, well-made monitor stands available for pretty cheap, though, so I wouldn't let that stop you from buying this!
